T-Shirt Design Contest

Are you artistic? Do you want to help us prepare for May 29? Then enter the TEDxEastBrunswickHighSchool t-shirt design contest! The design should be related to the event as well as to our theme of The Pursuit of Happiness. All designs should be created on CustomInk and must be submitted to TEDxEastBrunswickHighSchool@gmail.com no later than April 12. The winning design will be our official t-shirt and its creator will win a free ticket to the event! Email us with any questions you may have. (Note: Only open to students currently attending East Brunswick High School - sorry!) .
